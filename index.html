About Me

Hi! My name is Namuun. I am a Grade 12 student who is deeply passionate about science, especially biology and chemistry, and how they can be used to solve real-world problems. My interest in science didn’t just come from school, but from my own life experiences, which made me see it as something meaningful and powerful rather than just academic.

Over the years, I have pushed myself to go beyond the classroom by exploring topics like regenerative medicine, chemistry education, and scientific research. I’ve also worked on building a chemistry platform for high school students, where I create content and collaborate with a team to make science more accessible and engaging.

I believe in continuous growth, curiosity, and using knowledge to make a real impact. Whether it’s through research, projects, or sharing knowledge with others, I am committed to learning deeply and contributing creative and meaningful ideas.
